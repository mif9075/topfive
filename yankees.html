Top Five Best Yankees Players

  1. Babe Ruth

    • Career spanned 22 seasons. 1914-1935.

  2. Derek Jeter
    • Future Hall of Famer.
    • 3,463 hits.
    • 14× All-Star (1998–2002, 2004, 2006–2012, 2014).

  3. Lou Gehrig
    • Gehrig batted cleanup, increasing the pressure for pitchers to throw strikes to Ruth.

    • Gehrig was a legend. Too bad he is most known for his disease

  4. Mickey Mantle
    • Probably would of been the best ever if he hadnt got hurt.
    • 536 home runs.
    • Voted the American League's Most Valuable Player three times (1956–57, 1962)

  5. Joe DiMaggio
    • "Joltin' Joe"
    • "The Yankee Clipper"

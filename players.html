Top Five Best Baseball Players of All Time

  1. Babe Ruth
    • He was a great pitcher and hitter.
    • He is the Great BAMBINO.
    • He changed the game and dominated like no other player.

  2. Willie Mays
    • Best position player ever.
    • The only player with the talent to do everything.
    • Hit for power and average, great fielder, rocket arm, and running at the highest level.

  3. Hank Aaron
    • Greatest hitter of all time.
    • 755 home runs.
    • 3,771 career hits

  4. Ted Williams
    • Highest On-Base Percentage ever.
    • Won the triple crown in 1947.
    • Miss 6 years due to military service.

  5. Ty Cobb
    • Highest career batting average.
    • First to be voter into the Hall of Fame.
    • The meanest man to ever play the game.

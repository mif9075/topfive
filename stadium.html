Top Five Best Major League Baseball Stadium

  1. Fenway Park - Red Sox
    • Historical stadium.
    • Opened April 20th, 1912.
    • Remains the same.

  2. Wrigley Field - Cubs
    • Built in 1914
    • Playing host to Major League Baseball for the 105th season in 2019.
    • Host for the Cubs for the 103rd year.

  3. Yankee Stadium - Yankees
    • Iconic.
    • Home of the best sports team ever.
    • Design and team are universally known and equated with awesomeness.

  4. Dodgers Stadium - Dodgers
    • 56,000 seats capacity.
    • Moved from Brooklyn to Los Angeles, due to stadium construction.
    • Chavez Ravine

  5. AT&T Park - Giants
    • Now called Oracle Park.
    • 2008 Sports Facility of the Year by Sports Business Journal and Sports Business Daily.
    • The first privately financed ballpark in Major League Baseball since 1962.

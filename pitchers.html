Top Five Greatest Pitchers of All Time

  1. Nolan Ryan
    • The "Ryan Express".
    • He hold 55 Major League pitching records.
    • All-time strikeout leader, no-hitters with 7.

  2. Sandy Koufax
    • Willie Mays and Hank Aaron couln't hit against him.
    • Most dominant pitcher of the 1960s.
    • 1961-1966: No other pitcher can compete for best six year period.

  3. Cy Young
    • Freak of nature.
    • Great control and fantastic speed.
    • Imagine Nolan Ryan and Roger Clemens in one person.

  4. Walter Johnson
    • 417 wins, ERA of 2.17, 110 shutouts.
    • Struck out Ty Cobb three times in one game. Ty Cobb swore he couldn't even see Walter's fastball.
    • He never pitched hitters inside (he didn't want to hurt them). Maybe if he had, he would have been even better

  5. Randy Johnson
    • Most under-rated pitcher.
    • In approximately 4,000 innings pitched, he was able to strikout nearly 5,000 batters
    • He won 9 strikeout titles.
